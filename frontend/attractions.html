Iconic Landmarks

From island memorials to majestic palaces, Kanyakumari's heritage is a tapestry of spiritual depth and regal history.

Vivekananda Rock Memorial

A sacred island monument where Swami Vivekananda attained enlightenment. Accessible only by a scenic ferry ride.

Thiruvalluvar Statue

A 133-foot stone sculpture dedicated to the legendary Tamil poet and philosopher, standing proudly in the Laccadive Sea.

A breathtaking Gothic-style cathedral. Its pristine white structure glows against the backdrop of the deep blue ocean.

Padmanabhapuram Palace

The largest wooden palace in Asia, a masterpiece of indigenous Kerala architecture featuring granite and intricate teakwood carvings.
